# DZEN Quality Standards

**Purpose**: WHAT quality looks like for Dzen content
**Version**: 1.0 | **Updated**: January 5, 2026

---

## Quality Gates

| Stage | Metric | Threshold | Fail Action |
|-------|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Stage 1 | Phase 2 Score per episode | >= 70 | Regenerate episode (max 3) |
| Stage 2 | Article length | 15-25K chars | Manual review |
| Stage 3 | Phase 2 Score | >= 85 | Regenerate Stage 2 (max 2) |
| Stage 4 | Overall Score | >= 80/100 | Return to Stage 3 |
| Stage 5 | Checklist | 8+ checks | Manual review/rewrite |

## Phase 2 Components

1. **Perplexity** (20% weight): >= 75
2. **Sentence Variance** (20% weight): >= 70
3. **Colloquialism** (20% weight): >= 75
4. **Emotional Authenticity** (15% weight): >= 70
5. **Fragmentary** (15% weight): >= 50
6. **Repetition** (10% weight): >= 55

## DZEN GURU Rules (6 Critical)

1. **Dialogues in DASHES** (not quotes)
2. **Max 3 complex names**
3. **Alternating sentence lengths**
4. **NO AI clichés** (bottomless eyes, unruly curls)
5. **Character evolution shown**
6. **Read aloud naturally**

## 10-Point Quality Checklist

- First sentence hooks?
- Turning point at 30%?
- Climax at 60%?
- Resolution at 85%?
- Ending CLOSED?
- Dialogues 40-50%?
- NO AI-clichés?
- Character changed?
- Max 3 complex names?
- Reads naturally?

**Score: 8-10 = PUBLISH | 6-7 = REWRITE PARTS | <6 = FULL REWRITE**
