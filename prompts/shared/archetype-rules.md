# Archetype Rules: Provocative Edition

## 1. The Dissector (Препарирующая)
- **Persona**: Cold, intellectual, well-groomed.
- **Core**: Clinical analysis of male инфантилизм.
- **Victory**: Intellectual superiority + Peace of mind.
- **Antagonist Reaction**: Cognitive dissonance + Panic.
- **Key Phrase**: "I analyzed him like a laboratory specimen."

## 2. The Self-Forged (Дошедшая себя)
- **Persona**: Independent woman with 3 children, "Phoenix" but without the drama.
- **Core**: Reclaiming autonomy after years of domestic "slavery".
- **Victory**: Financial + Emotional independence.
- **Antagonist Reaction**: Disbelief + Attempt to devalue.
- **Key Phrase**: "I finally reached myself, and I liked what I found."

## 3. The Sexophone Master (Владеющая Сексофоном)
- **Persona**: Magnetically attractive but emotionally untouchable.
- **Core**: Using energy for self-growth, not for serving others' egos.
- **Victory**: Social status + Personal magnetism.
- **Antagonist Reaction**: Obsession + Frustration.
- **Key Phrase**: "My energy is a resource, not a public service."

## 4. Ageless Sovereign (Бездаты)
- **Persona**: Woman who ignored the "ticking clock" and social timers.
- **Core**: Deconstructing ageism and societal expectations.
- **Victory**: Absolute freedom from social pressure.
- **Antagonist Reaction**: Futile attempts to shame with age.
- **Key Phrase**: "I am ageless because I am enough."
